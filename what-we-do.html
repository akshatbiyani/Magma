Our focus is quality content

Magma Consultancy partners with and produces quality content for the world’s most exciting companies.

Our work is governed by two core beliefs.

We hold that quality content makes all the difference in attracting audiences, generating leads, and growing businesses. Content is the currency that companies are using to differentiate themselves and attract the right audiences.

We believe quality content can be the experience that drives business growth and profitability. It’s what helps you draw and retain clearly-defined audiences and forge long-term relationships with them. ‍
‍
Actually, we believe our content goes even further! Our insights-driven approach helps clients define themselves as formidable thought leaders and category definers. That may mean going from zero to thousands of engaged followers on LinkedIn, or having a refreshing website with original research and outstanding content.

Ways to work with us

Our content is crafted to help you achieve strategic goals and strengthen your core business. We are flexible and can adapt our services rapidly to meet your needs.

Understand

We gather in-depth knowledge about your company or industry.

Create

We plan and execute customised content and visuals for maximum impact.

Optimise

We go back to the drawing board to make changes if necessary.

How we help

We focus on driving conversion through the power of substantive content. This includes:

Thought leadership: Op-eds, blogs, and articles that act as thought partners to leadership.
Operational content: Social media posts, web copy, case studies, product guides, factsheets, interviews, and press releases.
Graphic design: We top it off with thoughtful and high-impact graphics, either as standalone pieces or in-post inserts.
Video Content: Engaging and dynamic video content that captures attention and drives engagement across platforms, from explainer videos to promotional clips.
Website Design: Creating user-friendly, visually appealing websites that resonate with your brand identity and enhance the user experience.
Branding: Comprehensive branding services, including brand strategy, visual identity, and messaging, to establish a strong and cohesive brand presence.
